3. Inactive Accounts

To keep our systems clean and protect your data, accounts that have been inactive for 12 months or more are automatically deactivated and follow the same deletion process described above (30-day grace period for hard deletion).

We define "inactive" as no login, no game played, and no app activity for 12 consecutive months.
